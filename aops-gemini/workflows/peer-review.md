# Peer Review Workflow

**Purpose**: Manage the review process for academic submissions including grants, fellowships, and journal articles.

**When to invoke**: User says "review this grant", "process reviewer comments", "evaluate this fellowship application", or similar.

## Core Workflow Steps

1. **Initial Review**: Read the submission and understand the key objectives and methods.
2. **Detailed Evaluation**: Assess the submission against the specific criteria for the scheme or journal.
3. **Formulate Feedback**: Draft constructive, evidence-based feedback for the authors/applicant.
4. **Scoring**: Assign scores that reflect the textual evaluation provided.
5. **Quality Check**: Review the feedback for tone, objectivity, and completeness.
6. **Submission**: Log into the relevant platform (RMS, mySNF, etc.) and submit the review.
7. **Task Completion**: Update the review task status and record any key learnings.

## Critical Rules

- **Objectivity**: Maintain a constructive and professional tone at all times.
- **Evidence-Based**: Ensure all scores and feedback are supported by evidence from the submission.
- **Conflict of Interest**: Declare any potential COIs before starting the review.
- **Deadlines**: Always keep track of submission deadlines and prioritize accordingly.
